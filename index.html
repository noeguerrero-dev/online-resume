About Me

As a multifaceted professional with a rich tapestry of experiences, I bring a unique blend of technical prowess and creative ingenuity to the digital landscape. My journey began in Brownsville, Texas, where, under the guidance of my hardworking parents, I learned the values of discipline, resilience, and integrity. Raised to be a man of both principle and faith, I embraced challenges from a young age, balancing rigorous academic pursuits with the demands of being a Division 1 college baseball pitcher. This dual commitment honed my focus and work ethic, qualities that have been pivotal in my career.

Following an unexpected end to my athletic journey due to an injury, I channeled my passion for competition and strategy into the realm of technology. Intrigued by video games as a child, I delved into coding and game development at 14, laying the foundation for a career that would later encompass web and app development, software creation, and digital marketing. By 21, I had founded RGV Web Design LLC in Edinburg, Texas, a testament to my entrepreneurial spirit and commitment to empowering local businesses through enhanced online presence and brand establishment.

Today, as a seasoned Web Developer, Designer, Software Developer, App Developer, Game Developer, and Marketing Director, I leverage a comprehensive skill set to drive digital transformations. My approach is hands-on and results-oriented, focusing on delivering tangible value and ensuring sustained success for the companies I partner with. Beyond professional engagements, I continue to explore new technologies and develop personal projects, fueled by an unwavering curiosity and a desire to contribute meaningfully to the ever-evolving digital landscape.
